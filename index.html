About Me

Create cool things. Be humble in doing so.

That’s always been a concept that I go by. Over the course of 19 years, I’ve been fortunate enough to work for companies big and small alike- from designing in-house advertising campaigns for one of the largest brands in the world, the US Navy, to product and apparel design for companies like ONE Industries and Upper Deck.

When I’m not on my design grind, I transform into a Jedi Master, training my 16 and 10 year old padawans in the ways of the Force at our Jedi Temple.

Currently, I'm learning web development to add to my design arsenal.
